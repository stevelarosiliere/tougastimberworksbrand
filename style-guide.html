07 / Photography Direction
Documentary.
Not Decorated.
Real light, real work, real dust. No staging, no stock photos, no lifestyle fakery. Show the shop as it is.
⚙
The Process
Milling, drying, planing in action
✋
The Hands
Close-ups of hands on wood, tools, grain
🌳
The Material
Raw logs, stacked lumber, end grain detail
🏠
The Shop
Wide shots of the mill, equipment, space
🙌
The People
Eric at work, candid, no posed portraits
✨
The Finish
Completed pieces, installed projects, details
Guidelines
  • Natural light preferred. Warm tones. Golden hour when possible.
  • Shallow depth of field for detail shots. Wide for environment.
  • Show sawdust, grain, texture. Let imperfection be the aesthetic.
  • Desaturate slightly in post. Warm shadows, not cool.
  • Never over-styled, never sterile, never stock-photo energy.
  • If it looks like a magazine ad, reshoot it.

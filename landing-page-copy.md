## 5. PROCESS SECTION

**Section Headline:** How It Works

### Step 1: Tell Us What You Need
Reach out with your project idea. A countertop for your kitchen, a dining table for twelve, a live edge mantle. We'll talk dimensions, species, budget, and timeline. No commitment, no pressure.

### Step 2: Pick Your Material
Come to the shop and walk the inventory. Pick the exact slab or boards for your piece. We'll talk through grain patterns, species characteristics, and what will work best for your space.

### Step 3: We Build It
Your piece gets built in our shop from the material you selected. We handle milling, joinery, sanding, and finishing. Typical lead time is 2 to 3 weeks. We'll send progress photos along the way.

### Step 4: Delivery and Install
We deliver throughout Connecticut, with a focus on Fairfield County. If your piece needs installation (mantles, countertops, floating shelves), we handle that too. Balance due on completion.
